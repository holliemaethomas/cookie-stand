Salmon Cookies

A home town favorite

We started baking salmon cookies for our friends that would visit from out of town. We would joke that everything in Seattle is about two things, salmon and coffee! One night I decided to mix in a small amount of left over Salmon into some peanutbutter cookies and BAM! instant classic. What started at home, in our kitchen, with friends has now become our family business.
